Tiago Gonçalves · 4259 4575 · tiago86g@gmail.com

My name is Tiago Gonçalves. I was born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, but I’m currently spending my days in Copenhagen, Denmark, where I live with my family. Besides trying to survive, I also work as a graphic designer, mostly with books, but also with a range of other stuff. I graduated from ESDI - College of Higher Education in Design at UERJ - Rio de Janeiro State University in 2010 but I have been working professionally as a designer since 2007. In 2011 I started working as an Art Director at a Brazilian publishing house called Azougue Editorial. Though it is not one of the biggest publishing houses, it constantly makes itself noticed for its cultural interventions, especially at the art scene. Besides books, I also do a wide range of freelance jobs, many of which you can see in this portfolio. This has mainly been with graphic design and exposition design.
